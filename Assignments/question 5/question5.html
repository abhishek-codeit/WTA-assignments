Importanceof an income statement

An income statement is a financial statement that summarizes the revenues, costs and expenses incurred during a specific period of time, usually a fiscal quarter or year. The income statement is also known as the profit and loss statement (P&L) or the statement of operations. It is one of the three key financial statements that report the financial performance of a company over a period of time. The other two key financial statements are the balance sheet and the cash flow statement.
